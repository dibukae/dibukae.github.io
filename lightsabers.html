Lightsaber colors

In the movies, most jedi have either a green or blue lightsaber (unless you are Samuel L. jackson and ask for a purple lightsaber), and all sith have a red lightsaber.

There are a lot more lightsaber colors available. We see a yellow lightsaber in The Rise of Skywalker but there are still more colors.

There can be cyan, white, orange, magenta, indigo, and black lightsabers. We see Ahsoka Tano using white lightsabers in the Rebels series and the Mandalorian series.

We also see a black lightsaber, called the Darksaber, in the Mandalorian series. The Darksaber is an ancient mandalorian weapon.

Whoever wins the Darksaber in battle is declared the ruler of Mandalore.
